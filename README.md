# Coffee-Shop-Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes coffee shop sales data to optimize staffing efficiency and product focus. Inspired by prior barista experience, the report identifies key patterns in peak hours and product performance to provide actionable business recommendations.

## Tools & Skills Applied
* **Problem Definition:** Defined critical questions regarding staffing, product mix, and store performance.
* **SQL Skills:** Processed transaction data and extracted hour values for time-based analysis.
* **Tableau Visualization:** Designed interactive dashboards and heatmaps to reveal sales trends.
* **Analytical Thinking** Translated data findings into operational strategies suggestions and structured reporting.

## Key Questions
- How can staff scheduling be optimized based on peak and off-peak traffic patterns?
- Which product categories are most popular, and how does demand change throughout the day?
- Which store location performs best, and are location-based strategies necessary?

## Key Insights (Summary)
- Customer demand peaks between **6â€“9 AM**, with order volume more than double that of other hours, indicating the need for increased staffing during morning shifts.
- Coffee and brewed tea products dominate morning sales, while **hot chocolate shows relatively stable demand throughout the day**.
- Revenue trends across store locations follow a **highly similar pattern**, suggesting that seasonality and time-of-day effects are more influential than location-specific factors.
